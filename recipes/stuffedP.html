Stuffed Peppers

A best of both worlds favorite

Stuffed peppers are a staple recipe in American cuisine. They're simple to make and affordable for a big family to eat. They combine meat and peppers with iterchangable ingredients for a blast of flavor you wont forget!

Ingredients

  • Peppers
  • Ground Beef
  • Shredded Cheese
  • White rice
  • Parsely

Steps

  1. Cook meat separately,(in oven preferably)
  2. mix meat with rice and parsely
  3. Stuff mix into peppers
  4. bake peppers for 20 minutes at 350*F

Enjoy!
