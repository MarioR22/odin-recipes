Quesadillas

Mexican powerhouse enters the ring

The quesadilla, is a Mexican tradition, dating back centuries. This recipe will put a southwestern spin on the beheamouth and give it a kick. Only the first two ingredients are required and those are interchangable in their class. Enjoy amigos and amigas!

Ingredients

  • Flat Tortillas
  • Cheese, your choice, (preferably shredded)
  • Protein
  • Peppers
  • Onions
  • Hot sauce

Steps

  1. cook protein however you'd like. (make sure internal temperature reaches 165 degrees minimum!)
  2. Add peppers and onions to protein and sautee.
  3. add sauteed items to tortilla with cheese and fold in half
  4. Throw quesadilla on skillet and cook on high, flipping when golden-brown.

Enjoy!
