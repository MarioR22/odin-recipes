Ramen

Dattebayo! The Japanese have invaded the US!

Add a soup item to your diet with ramen.Enjoy this ancient Japanese recipe catching fire in the states with your own preferred ingredients. Its fun to eat and delicious.

Ingredients

  • Noodles of your choice
  • Pork or what you desire
  • egg
  • bamboo
  • ginger

Steps

  1. boil noodles and eggs separately
  2. boil bamboo and ginger together
  3. sear pork or other protein
  4. boil water and add ginger/bamboo combo
  5. add noodles and turn off heat
  6. add pork or other protein and let sit until safe to eat
